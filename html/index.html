Our patients are at the heart of everything we do at UF Health. Our top priority is to provide the highest quality health care and the best possible experience for every patient, family member and visitor who walks through our doors. Hospitality and service are a state of mind — an approach that’s reflected in all our actions throughout the day. It starts with the way we support and treat each other as colleagues and internal customers. In turn, our relationships create the foundation for how we serve those who trust us with their care. We can make a positive impact with every interaction. By giving our caring attention to each customer — whether it is a patient, visitor or coworker — we create an environment where people feel acknowledged, understood, safe and valued.
